Extracurricular Activities

Explore my involvement in various extracurricular activities that have enriched my university experience.

Shanghai Summer School 2024

I represented Malaysia at the Shanghai Summer School 2024, where I explored global perspectives on education, technology, and culture while networking with international peers from diverse backgrounds. The program enriched my cross-cultural communication skills and broadened my worldview.

Semarak Siswa Madani @ Turkiye 2024

I served as the treasurer for the Semarak Siswa Madani @ Turkiye 2024 program, a student leadership and cultural exchange initiative. I managed finances, ensured transparency in budgeting, and optimized resource allocation to support activities, travel logistics, and accommodation. This experience strengthened my financial management skills and enhanced my ability to work under pressure in an international setting.

Malam Permata Gemilang 2024

As the director of Malam Permata Gemilang 2024, I led and oversaw the entire event, coordinating a team, handling logistics, and ensuring the smooth execution of the annual college dinner for final-year students. This role enhanced my event management, problem-solving, and leadership skills while contributing to a memorable evening of tributes, performances, and awards.
